A leading provider of high-quality Casters & Wheels

Ruibang is a manufacturer and distributor of casters and wheels. We provide the finest casters and wheels at the lowest prices available.

Jiaxing Ruibang hardware tools Co.,Ltd was established in 2009, located at Haiyan, Jiaxing City, Zhejiang Province - a well-known production base of miscellaneous hardware accessories in China. Our Company has about 100 employees and a manufactory occupying over 15,000 square meters.

Ruibang is a professional manufacturer which specialized in producing medical appliance casters, industrial casters, furmiture casters, grey rubber casters, nylon casters, light-duty casters and heavy-duty casters with load capacity of 1800 pounds. Our products are widely acclaimed both in the domestic and overseas markets, especially are selling well in the Europe, America, Middle East and Southeast Asia.
