How to find the right caster for your products

Using casters can reduce the work force ang increase the work efficiency. The right caster should be chosen by the manner of application condition and request (for example facility laborsaving, durability).The factor must be considered as follows:

A, The loading capacity

Load: =E+Z/M
T=each caster load
E=the weight of the vehicle
Z=the weight of moving object
M=efficient load casters quality(the position and different heavy distribution must be considered).

B, Agility

B-1, The part of turn(frame turn ,wheel rolling)should choose the mini. Frictiuon material, or the accessories which be made by special technology, such as pin, ball bearing or quenched in order that the casters will be easy and durable.
B-2, The more bigger the eccentricity is the better the agility becomes.
B-3, The bigger wheel diameter is the more laborsaving is becomes, and can protect the floor surface perfectly ,the bigger wheel run much slowly ,not easily getting headed and distorted ,and more durable than the small. It is better to choose the bigger diameter wheel when the fitting height is enough.

C, Moving speed

The request of caster speed: at the normal temperature and on the smooth floor, the speed should be lower　than 4km/h of walk and there is static interval during the work time.

D, Applied environment

The floor material ,barrier leftover and the special environment, such as scrap iron ,high or low temperature,great acidity and alkalescency, oil liquor chemistry solvent andante-static-electricity, should be considered when choose the caster, caster used in special environment should be made of special materials.

E, Fitting notice

Plate; fitted plate must be level ,hard and firm.
Rigid: the direction of two wheels must be accordant and paralle.
Threaded: must be fitted with spring shim to prevent from loosening.
