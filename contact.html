Welcome to Contact Ruibang Hardware Tools

Jiaxing ruibang hardware tools Co.,Ltd was established in 2009, located at Haiyan, Jiaxing City, Zhejiang Province, one of the well-known production bases of miscellaneous hardware accessories in China. Our Company has about 100 employees and a manufactory occupying over 15,000 square meters.

Ruibang Caster is a professional manufacturer which specialized in producing medical appliance casters, industrial casters, furmiture casters, grey rubber casters, nylon casters, light-duty casters and heavy-duty casters with load capacity of 1800 pounds. By several years of development,we own a group of specialized personnel in R&D, production, management, quality control and after sales. Our products are widely acclaimed both in the domestic and overseas markets, especially are selling well in the Europe, America, Middle East and Southeast Asia.

With sincerity, faith, diligence, excellent service, high quality products and reasonable prices, we always establish long-term business relations with our customers. Welcome your contacting with us for any inquiry.
