All kinds of casters have their own characteristics and functions

Nowadays, with the development of science and technology, the equipment is also more and more multifunctional and high utilization rate, casters become an indispensable component. Therefore, the development of casters also becomes much professional and turns into a special industry.

Casters are an umbrella name, which including swivel casters and rigid casters. The rigid ones have no rotational structure, can’t rotate freely. But the structures of swivel casters allow rotating 360 degrees. Usually two casters are used together, such as a hand cart. Its structure is two rigid casters in the front and two swivel casters close to the armrest at back.

This kind of products is mainly used in industry, healthcare, homecare and so on. Surely, different uses of casters also use different materials. Our common materials are PU, Rubber, PA, TPU.

All kinds of casters have their own characteristics and functions.

In order to adapt the hospital requirements, medical casters are operate lightly, flexible steering, elastic, special super mute, wear-resistant, prevent winding and Chemical Resistance, etc.

Industrial casters are mainly used in the factory or mechanical equipment. The whole products should be of high impact resistance and strength. Single casters made by Advanced import reinforced nylon, Super PU and rubber can be selected.

Super-market casters are specialized products which to adapt the move requirement. Light and flexible are the most important.

And furniture casters are produced in order to adapt the need of low barycenter and high bearing.

Using casters can reduce the work force and increase the work efficiency. The right caster should be chosen by the manner of application condition and request. The factors must be considered include loading capacity, agility, moving speed, applied environment and fitting notice. Every buyer should choose carefully according to their own conditions. A small factor also will affect the operation of equipment. And each caster has different service life, users should pay attention to update.

We cannot imagine living without casters. It has into our life.
